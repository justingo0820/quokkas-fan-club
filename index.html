So, you want a cup of Joe, but where do you go?

Here in Portland, we're blessed with an abundance of coffee shops. There are so many options. How on earth do you decide where to go?

Let's go through my list of my favorite shops, why, and where you can find them!

Stumptown

  • How many locations?: 4
  • Cost per medium drip?: $2
  • Claim to Fame?: Nitro Cold Brew
  • Available in stores?: Yep! Most local grocery stores.

Sisters

  • How many locations?: 2
  • Cost per medium drip?: $2.50
  • Claim to Fame?: Being named after a mountain range
  • Available in stores?: No

Barista

  • How many locations?: 4
  • Cost per medium drip?: $3.25
  • Claim to Fame?: Above average cold brew
  • Available in stores?: No
